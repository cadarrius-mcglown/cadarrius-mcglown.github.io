About Me

I became fascinated with computers at a very young age. For fun, I would build custom gaming computers which I would optimize and overclock for peak performance benchmarking. I would also create custom scripts to run alongside my favorite online games such as runescape and counterstrike. This interest in the overall theory of computing led me to persure a B.E. in Computer Engineering from the Herff college of Engineering. I have a very broad range of interests within the field ranging from low level hardware to high level software. I am currently working in industry developing large scale software for business optimization. Software has become a love of mine! I currently am pursuing a M.S. in Applied Computer Science where I will be focusing on Software Engineering design patterns.
